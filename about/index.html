About

Posted on Feb 28, 2019

我，2022年的時候17歲。

從小學畫，直到2021年的時候被好友 @need_some_latte 拉進富邦青少年影像發聲學院，開始拍片，也開始喜歡影像。 喜歡以奇怪的角度觀察人或是其他在地球上的東西，喜歡街拍，由於只有一顆85的定焦鏡，可以看得更遠;喜歡速寫，出門畫畫就帶著黃色封面的速寫本、灌滿防水墨的鋼筆和小盤的水彩。

I was 17 years old in 2020.

Learn drawing since my childhood, in 2021 I got suggested and join “Fubon Young Voice Academy” by @need_some_latte, started to make films, and started to like images. like to observe people and other things in the world from strange angles, like street photography, I have only an 85mm lens, so I can see farther; I like to sketch, going out with a yellow-covered sketchbook, a fountain pen which filled with waterproof ink, and a little watercolor palette.
